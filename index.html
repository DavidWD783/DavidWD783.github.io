What you need to know about the spring classics in cycling...

The spring classics in cycling cover the following races:

  • Omloop Het Nieuwsblad - Belgium, February 27
  • Kuurne-Brussels-Kuurne - Belgium, February 28
  • Milan-San Remo(La Classicissima) - Italy, March 19
  • Gent-Wevelgem - Belgium, March 27
  • Ronde van Vlaanderen(De Ronde) - Belgium, April 3
  • Paris-Roubaix("Queen of the Classics" and "Hell of the North") - France, April 10
